MPLAN Magazine is an independent publication working in the fields of urbanism, architecture and everyday life. Forever in search of overlooked moments within registers including Matter0, Projects0 and Letters0, it draws inspiration from the street and often utilises Address a space0 and Notes0 to activate the page. The magazine's oeuvre varies from essays and critiques to field notes and speculative ideas.
